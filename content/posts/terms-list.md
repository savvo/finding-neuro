# Finding Neuro Terms list #1                                                                     


## Alexithymia
Difficulty identifying, understanding and describing your emotions / the emotions of others

## Masking
The process of suppressing your natural feelings and behaviours, usually to try and fit in with social norms or to get through a situation (drama club- sbsk)

Rejection sensitive dysphoria
Experiencing an intense emotional reaction to perceived rejection / criticism, regardless of how small 
Rejection Sensitivity: Explainer and Advice

Time blindness
Difficulty in perceiving and managing time, often leading to challenges with estimating how long tasks take, prioritising, and meeting deadlines

Hyperfocus
An intense fixation on an interest or an activity for a long period of time, often losing track of surroundings and finding it difficult to give attention to anything else

Burnout
A state of mental, emotional, and physical exhaustion caused by a long period of stress, often from being in a high-pressure environment for an extended time

Misophonia
Experiencing an intense emotional reaction (often anger) to certain sounds 

Thought-loops
Cycles of repetitive thinking that keep returning to the same topics or concerns, usually without a set end point or resolution



Procrastination
Becoming stuck delaying tasks / decisions, even if knowing that this delay will have negative consequences

Dopamine
A neurotransmitter that is important in regulating motivation, enjoyment and reward 

Maladaptive behaviour
A behaviour which provides some short-term relief but does more harm than good in the long-term

Distress-tolerance
The ability to endure and overcome emotional discomfort without using harmful coping strategies

Positive deviance:
Reflecting on times when, despite challenging circumstances, things worked out well, to see what can be learned or replicated.
If we only manage to do something one day a week, it’s common to feel shame and beat ourselves up over the 6 days we didn’t manage it, rather than looking at why and how we managed to succeed on that one day.
