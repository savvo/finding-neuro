Stress responses: How the body and brain handle perceived threats / dysregulation to try to keep us safe

## Fight
    To confront (aggressive, defensive)
    Example - Desire to shout someone after sensory overload

## Flight
    To escape / withdraw
    Example - Avoiding a daunting task with distractions

## Freeze
    Inability to act
    Example - Analysis paralysis

## Fawn
    Appeasing others
    Example - People pleasing, having a hard time saying no

## Flop
    To mentally or physically collapse
    Example - Blanking or complete loss of motivation while overloaded
